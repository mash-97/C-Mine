								About CS: (Cricket Scoreboard)
		
		Simply Cricket Scoreboard an administrtional purposed program, but from the use it is more 
	than that. Because it let us to keep store and manage all the information related a match, teams    	and players. We can later use or perform some general operations like search which player has       	maximum runs, average or which team has more wins over another team and many more. 
	
		But in this program all of the possible features weren't programmed except some common features. 		Moreover, the way the program was developed, it is expectable that it will be easy to extend later.
